The People\u2019s Potato
\n
The People\u2019s Potato is a vegan soup kitchen at Concordia University \u2013 a student initiated project that was founded in 1999 in order to address student poverty. They offer by donation meals each day of the week during the Fall and Winter semesters. They serve more than 400 meals daily to students, community members with the help of their dedicated volunteers. They are committed to educating about healthy cooking and food politics and to broader goals of social and environmental justice.
