CCSA is Most Academically\xa0Focused!
\t
\n\t\t
At last night\u2019s ASFA Spring Ball, the Concordia Classics Student Association took home the title of \u201cMost Academically Focused Member Association!\u201d We\u2019re thrilled to have been recognized for all our work organizing Brown Bag and Guest Lectures (as well as various other student and professor presentations) this year. And we want to thank all Classics faculty, CMLL staff, and, most importantly, participating Classics students (!) for helping make it a super productive and successful year.\xa0
\n
Best of luck on finals/enjoy the start of your summer, as the case may be!
\n
-The CCSA
