     \n        <!-- Output the messages -->\n\t\t    \t\t      \n\t\t    <!-- Rendering the tabs to view and edit nodes -->\n\t\t    \t\t      \t\t    \n      </div>\n    </div>\n    \n    <div field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><p><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">After a referendum question where 86% of students voted in favor of\xa0using\xa0of a portion of the Student Space, Accessible Education &amp; Legal Contingency Fund\xa0for the creation and expansion of predominantly student-run food systems projects on campus, the CSU developed a Food Special Project Funding Policy to better access the fund. If you are an undergraduate student or group who wish\xa0to create or expand a student-run\xa0food-system project on campus, please have a look at the CSU <strong>Food System Special Project Funding Policy.</strong></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal"><br/><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">Preamble</b></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">In 2013 and 2014, two referendum questions assessed the will of the Concordia Student Union\u2019s members to support and expand a predominantly student run food system on campus. Respectively, the two referendum questions adopted were as follows:</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">\xb7<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>Would you like to see the CSU actively support the new affordable, sustainable, student-run food service initiatives on campus?</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpLast" style="text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">\xb7<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>The <i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">Student Space, Accessible Education &amp; Legal Contingency Fund</i> has been accumulating large amounts of student money for 10 years. The appropriate use of these funds as outlined in the by-laws is open to interpretation. Do you as a member of the Concordia Student Union approve of the use of a portion of this fund for the creation and expansion of predominantly student-run food systems projects on campus?</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal">\xa0</p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">As a result of these successful referendums, the CSU has put in place the following policy to regulate the use of the <i style="mso-bidi-font-style:\nnormal">Student Space, Accessible Education &amp; Legal Contingency Fund</i> to help facilitate the growth of a student-run community food system on campus.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">\xa0</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 6pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:\nnormal">Definitions</b></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="margin-left: 0cm;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">Throughout this policy, the terms used are defined as set out in the Concordia Student Union\u2019s Standing Regulations, other than as follows:</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 35.85pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">a.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>\u201cFinancial Office\u201d means the CSU\u2019s financial team, composed by the CSU\u2019s Financial Coordinator, its General Manager, and its accountant.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 35.85pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">b.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>\u201cPredominantly student-run groups\u201d means groups whose membership is predominantly student or whose board is predominantly student-run, or both; that a majority of staff will be students or that a clear emphasis on student involvement can be demonstrated.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 35.85pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">c.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>\u201cResponsible Coordinator\u201d means the member of the CSU\u2019s executive selected by the Executive Committee to be responsible for the application of this policy and for supporting organizations that are seeking funding through the Food System SPF.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 63.8pt; text-indent: -63.8pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;"><span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>i.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>By default, the Responsible Coordinator will be the CSU\u2019s Sustainability Coordinator, unless the executive specifically decides otherwise.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">d.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>\u201cSustainable Food System\u201d means <span style="background: white none repeat scroll 0% 0%;">a collaborative network that integrates sustainable food production, processing, distribution, consumption and waste management in order to enhance the environmental, economic and social health of a particular place.</span></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">e.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>\u201cSocial Inclusion Costs\u201d refers to costs related to issues around the physical accessibility of spaces, sliding-scale pricing or other considerations for those who are financially overburdened, support for student-parents, or gender equality / anti-oppression.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 35.85pt;">\xa0</p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 6pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:\nnormal">General Provisions and Criteria</b></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 12pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">1.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>The purpose of the Food System Special Project Funding is to act as a grant for predominantly student run organizations on Concordia\u2019s campuses so as to replace their need to rely exclusively on third party loans. However, funds available for each project may be capped by the CSU, based on the number of applications, the perceived needs of the project, or for any other reason deemed necessary by its governing bodies.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 12pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">2.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>The CSU must act to ensure that these funds will, above all, be used for the benefit of CSU members. However, it is encouraged that projects, where possible, also benefit the community-at-large. Benefits to the community-at-large may take the form of access to the service, and may also be related to the sustainability practices of the foodservice involved (sourcing, environmental impact, considerations for marginalized groups, etc.).</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">3.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>The CSU\u2019s role throughout this process is to act as a support to organizations that are seeking funding through the Food System SPF, by offering information, guidance, and assistance throughout the process. The Responsible coordinator will also be responsible for working closely with the CSU\u2019s Financial Office to ensure that the project has\xa0 firm financial basis and plan.</span></span><br style="mso-special-character:line-break"/>\n\xa0</p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 17.85pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">4.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>To be eligible to apply for this funding, the project must fulfill the following criteria:</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 54pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">a.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>be predominantly student run and clearly focused on contributing to a sustainable food system at Concordia. Intend to remain predominantly student run over the long-term;</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 54pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">b.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>include educating the community on food justice, food politics, and/or opportunities to improve our current food system as one of its goals;</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 54pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">c.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>\xa0contribute to environmental, social, and economic sustainability;</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 54pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">d.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>be accessible to the Concordia university community and community at large, with a specific emphasis on the community geographically surrounding the university\u2019s campuses;</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 54pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">e.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>focus on the well-being of students rather than the generation of financial profits;</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 54pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">f.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>be able to demonstrate a significant level of advancement in its planning and preparation and have the realistic potential of being achieved in the timeframe it has prepared. This should include substantive documentation, including early surveys and studies, showing the project\u2019s viability.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">5.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>It is expected that additional sources of revenue and funding will be vigorously sought out by the applicant, in proportion to the applicant(s) resources and capacities to do so.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">6.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>Ideally, projects should involve multiple partners, primarily from organizations and individuals already working on campus, although external partners are both welcome and encouraged in certain cases.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 12pt 17.85pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">7.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>Projects should be able to demonstrate the active involvement of a clearly established promoter group whom the CSU can be reasonably confident will follow the project through to completion.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">8.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>Special considerations will be made for projects that involved social inclusion costs. These considerations may involve the granting of additional funds.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal">\xa0</p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom: 6pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:\nnormal">Application and Approval Process</b></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">1.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0\xa0\xa0\xa0 </span>Any group who believes that their project meets these eligibility criteria and that wishes to apply for a CSU Food System Special Project Funding must contact the CSU\u2019s Sustainability Coordinator. Should the Sustainability Coordinator not be designated as the Responsible Coordinator for this policy, they will connect the group with the Responsible Coordinator.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">2.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>The applicant must begin the process by discussing their project with the Responsible Coordinator, who will assist them in beginning to develop their application.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">3.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Once a group has completed the CSU\u2019s Food System SPF Application Form (available at the CSU office and through its website), the Responsible Coordinator will ensure that the application is properly completed and that the project falls within the established eligibility criteria.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">4.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Completed applications must be submitted before the second Thursday of October to be considered during the fall semester, and before the second Thursday of February for consideration in the winter semester.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">5.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Should the Responsible Coordinator find the application to be incomplete or inadequate, they may return it to the applicant(s) with comments and requests for additional information, refinements, modifications, or any other changes required to aid in its consideration. Such changes may include the need to seek out additional external funding or additional support or endorsements for the project from either external or on campus groups.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">6.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Once the Coordinator is satisfied that the application is complete, they will submit it to the CSU Council\u2019s Sustainability Committee for consideration. The Sustainability and Finance Committees must meet, and may choose to invite the applicant(s) to participate in their deliberations on the project.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">7.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>The Committees are responsible for reviewing and judging the merit of all submitted applications. Merit must be assessed based on the project\u2019s realistic likelihood of success, the demonstrated preparedness of the team for its application, the project\u2019s contributions to developing a sustainable food system at Concordia, its adherence to the eligibility criteria set out in this policy, and by any other criteria or consideration established by the Concordia Student Union in its policies, regulations, or by resolution duly adopted by its Council of Representatives, by referendum, or by its General Assembly.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">8.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Once the Sustainability and Finance Committees are satisfied as to the application\u2019s merit, they will send the application on to the CSU Council of Representatives.\xa0 <span style="background: white none repeat scroll 0% 0%;">The project leader(s) will be contacted and asked to present the project to Council, which will make the final decision to grant the funding request or not.</span></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">9.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>The group\u2019s application for Food System Special Project Funding requires a 2/3 majority vote in favour to be approved by Council.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">10.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; It may also reject the funding request altogether.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">11.<span style="font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Council\u2019s deliberations must take into account all relevant bylaws, regulations, positions, and/or resolutions adopted by the CSU\u2019s members at a duly held General Assembly, Special General Assembly, or referendum.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">12.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Should the application be accepted by Council, it will then be referred to the CSU\u2019s Fund Committee for ratification, as set out in Special By-Law J.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">13.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Council may, at its discretion, offer additional support (such as assistance in developing financial infrastructure, help acquiring a project manager, etc.) for the project.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">14.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>There is no need for Council to seek ratification by the Fund Committee for any portions of the additional support offered as long as they do not imply any use of funds from the <i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">Student Space, Accessible Education &amp; Legal Contingency Fund.</i></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">15.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>The Fund Committee\u2019s ratification of Council\u2019s decision requires a 4/5 majority vote to be adopted.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 6pt;">\xa0</p>\n\n<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 6pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">Accountability for the Funds</b></span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin-left: 18pt; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>The CSU Council is responsible for putting in place specific accountability measures, based on recommendations prepared by the Responsible Coordinator, the Sustainability Committee, and the Finance Committee, when it makes the decision to approve funds for a Food System SPF.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 18pt; The Committee may choose to reverse the Coordinator\u2019s decision and mandate them to continue working with the applicant(s) in preparing their application for its for consideration.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 17.85pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">3.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>The Sustainability Committee has the power to reject an application based on a lack of merit or if they decide that it does not fit within the eligibility criteria established by this policy, or by any other policy, regulation, or duly adopted resolution of the Concordia Student Union.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 17.85pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">4.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>The Committee\u2019s decision to reject an application (or to ratify the Coordinator\u2019s decision to do so) must be included in its next regular report to Council, with substantive details of the reasoning for that rejection. Their decision must be ratified by the CSU\u2019s Council of Representatives to be binding. The Council may choose to reverse the Committee\u2019s decision, and mandate the Responsible Coordinator to continue working with the applicant(s) in preparing their application for its consideration.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 0.0001pt 17.85pt; text-indent: -17.85pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">5.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Council\u2019s decision to reject an application is final and cannot be appealed, other than to the Judicial Board on the grounds of substantive procedural faults in their deliberation.</span></span></p>\n\n<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="margin: 12pt 0cm 12pt 18pt; text-indent: -18pt;"><span style="font-family:arial,helvetica,sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 12px;">6.<span style="font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; line-height: normal; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-feature-settings: normal; font-language-override: normal; font-kerning: auto; font-synthesis: weight style; font-variant: normal;">\xa0 </span>Should the Fund Committee fail to ratify the decision of Council for any reason whatsoever, only a duly convened referendum pursuant to the By-Laws and CSU regulations can forgo the ratification of the Committee.
