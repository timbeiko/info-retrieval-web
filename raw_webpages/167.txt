id="content" role="main"><div class="post-1656 page type-page status-publish hentry" id="post-1656"><h1 class="entry-title">Travel and Expenses</h1><div class="entry-content"><p><strong>University Policy for Travel and Other Allowable Expenses</strong></p><p>Two copies of a \u201cGrant Acceptance form\u201d will be enclosed with a letter informing you that you have been awarded a PD grant. Please read this form carefully and keep a copy on-hand for future reference. Don\u2019t forget to sign one copy and return it to CUPFA within 30 days of receiving your grant notification.</p><p>All of your expenses must be in accordance with University policy in order to be eligible for reimbursement. If this policy is not followed Financial Services will not reimburse you. Please read the Policy for Travel and other Allowable Expenses carefully \xa0<a href="http://www.cupfa.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/CupfaApril2015CFO-3-1.pdf">Policy for travel and other allowable expenses</a></p><p><strong>Paying Professionals</strong><br/>\nNEVER pay someone directly; they must be paid through the University. If hiring an individual or an organization (e.g., technicians, musicians, studio rental), you must complete a cheque requisition form and the University will pay the individual/organization. If you make payment yourself you will not be reimbursed. Contact your budget officer to receive cheque requisition forms.</p><p><b id="yui_3_13_0_rc_1_1_1381939816189_8335">Paying Concordia Students </b><br clear="none"/> If you received a PD Grant and are hiring Concordia students you must fill out a RA contract and the student(s) must fill out a TRAC Union membership form. They will still be paid directly from the CUPFA PDA account. Contact your Budget Officer for more information.<br clear="none"/> <b id="yui_3_13_0_rc_1_1_1381939816189_8334"><br clear="none"/> Steps for paying a Concordia Student</b><br clear="none"/> <br clear="none"/> Make sure you read the TRAC Contract Instructions carefully<br clear="none"/> <br clear="none"/> 1) The Concordia student must fill out a\xa0TRAC membership form<br clear="none"/> 2) Fill out the contract, sign it\xa0and have the Concordia student/Research Assistant sign it<br clear="none"/> 3) Return the form to your budget office along with a void cheque for Payroll (so the money can be deposited directly into the student\u2019s account.)<br clear="none"/> 4) Your budget officer inputs the\xa0CUPFA\xa0budget codes<br clear="none"/> 5) The completed contract and void cheque will be forwarded to payroll<br clear="none"/> 6) The TRAC membership form must be forwarded to TRAC<br clear="none"/> <br clear="none"/> <b>Please download all the forms</b><br clear="none"/> <a href="http://www.cupfa.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/notice-of-hire-oct13.pdf">Research Grant Employee Notice of Hire.</a><br/>\n<a href="http://www.cupfa.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/trac-ra-contract-instructions-oct13.pdf">TRAC Contract RA Instructions</a><br clear="none"/> <a href="http://www.cupfa.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/trac-union-membership-form-oct13.doc">Appendix B.Trac Union Membership form</a><br clear="none"/><a href="http://www.cupfa.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/NEWtrac-ra-2013-2014-contract-en_1-oct13.xls"> TRAC Contract (Excel or Numbers)</a></p><p>Please note that these forms can be downloaded from the HR website:<br/>\n<a href="http://www.concordia.ca/hr.html" target="_blank" title="HR">http://www.concordia.ca/hr.html</a></p><p><strong>Expenses over $1000</strong><br/>\nThe purchase of supplies or miscellaneous expenses in excess of $1000.00 must be done through Purchasing Services.</p><hr/><p>TRAVEL EXPENSES</p><p>If you have paid for your airline ticket yourself make sure you save your BOARDING PASSES and submit them with your receipt and expense report. Otherwise the University may not reimburse you.</p><p><strong>Concordia University Travel Voucher</strong><br/>\nMembers should consider taking advantage of University travel vouchers to pay for an airline ticket in advance. \xa0Before you book your trip, contact your Budget Officer and they will give you an account code that you must provide when you book your ticket with one of Concordia University\u2019s officially recognized travel agencies. \xa0A completed travel voucher must then be sent to your Budget Officer within 24 hours \xa0of booking the trip. \xa0The name of your Budget Officer appears at the bottom of the contract you signed upon acceptance of your grant. \xa0If you cannot find this important document, please call CUPFA and Karen or Manon will provide the information for you.<br/>\n<a href="http://www.cupfa.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/cupfatravel-voucher-form-2.pdf">Download Travel Voucher \u2013 pdf</a></p><p><strong>The following are the University\u2019s preferred travel agencies:</strong></p><p>Voyages Foreman Ideal<br/>\n514-342-9554<br/>\n<a href="http://www.groupeideal.ca/GroupeIdealContactus.html">www.groupeideal.ca</a></p><p>Norko International Travel<br/>\n514-871-8888<br/>\n<a href="http://www.norkotravel.com/">www.norkotravel.com</a></p><p>Uniglobe Voyages Lexus<br/>\n514-397-9221, ext. 230 or 242<br/>\n<a href="http://www.voyageslexus.com/">www.voyageslexus.com</a></p><p>Travel arrangements may be made through any other travel agency for a lower or equal fare but only after obtaining a written quote from one of the University\u2019s official travel agencies. This quote must be included with the expense report at the time of submission for reimbursement.</p><p><em>Note:</em> if travel by train or air is being claimed, the original boarding pass is required.</p><p>The university will accept alternate travel arrangements that the claimant made abroad.</p><p>Flight-cancellation insurance and health insurance are allowable expenditures. Receipts for such expenses should be kept and indicated on the claim form.</p><p>Vehicle rental may only be claimed if it is less expensive than economy airfare or if the destination is inaccessible by air or train.</p><p>Forms most commonly used: Expense reports, travel voucher.</p></div></div><div id="#top">\xab <a href="#top">Back to Top</a></div></div></div><div class="widget-area"
