Organization Booking Officer recognized by the University.</p>\n\n<p>Reservations by a recognized student group will only be considered if the event date falls within the then-current academic year.</p>\n\n<p>Reserved space may only be used by the individual or group which reserved it. It is prohibited to transfer a reservation to another individual or group without prior notice to, and written authorization</p>\n\n<p>The Requestor understands and agrees that a booking request is subject to the CSU\u2019s prior review and approval and that during this review and approval process:</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 The CSU may, at its sole and absolute discretion, decline an Event or conditionally approve an Event based on the Requestor\u2019s respect of certain conditions.</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 Further information regarding the Event may be required. It is the responsibility of the Requestor to provide the CSU with accurate, complete and up-to-date information within the delay set forth by the DSA, and to inform the CSU of any new or relevant information regarding the Event that may occur after receiving approval from the CSU to hold the Event, failing which the request may be declined or the approval of the Event rescinded, as applicable;</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 Note that any new or relevant information received after approval will entail a revision of the Event and, as such, the Event remains subject to an additional approval;</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 The University may determine that Security is required during the Event, at the Requestor\u2019s cost;</p>\n\n<hr/><p>The Requestor recognizes and agrees that:</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 Until the Requestor receives the CSU\u2019s written confirmation that its booking request is approved, the Event will not take place and the space is not booked;</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 The Event location may not be advertised until receiving written confirmation of the booking of the space;</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 The CSU reserves the right to suspend future booking privileges for any Requestor who fails to provide complete and accurate information when submitting the booking request or who fails to update such information as provided in this booking request;</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 It will pay all Event-related costs and fees invoiced to it by the CSU and University;</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 It must and will advise the CSU if registration fees or donation fees University Space (VPS-24)</p>\n\n<p>- Policy on the Recognition of Student Organizations and the Use of University Space (VPS-10)</p>\n\n<p>- Security Policy (VPS-20)</p>\n\n<p>- Policy on the Display of Posters (VPIRSG-1)</p>\n\n<p>- Policy concerning Minors on Campus (VPS-8)</p>\n\n<hr/><p><strong>Alcohol-related events</strong></p>\n\n<p>If the Requestor wants to serve/sell alcohol at the Event, a \u201cWaiver Form for Alcohol Related Events\u201d at least twenty (20) business days prior to the Event. Any costs incurred for catering will be paid by the Requestor.</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 If the Event is self-catered, comply with Procedures for Sale or Service of Food on Campus and, if applicable, complete and return to the DSA a \u201cWaiver Form for Self-Catered Events\u201d and comply with the requirements described therein, including requesting and receiving all applicable permits, such as the Special Event permit from the Minist\xe8re de l\u2019agriculture, P\xeacheries et aliminentation de Qu\xe9bec (\u201c<strong>MAPAQ</strong>\u201d). It is the responsibility of the Requestor to obtain all applicable permits.</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 If the Requestor fails to secure any required permits, the Requestor will not be allowed to self-cater the Event.</p>\n\n<p>\u2022 At least two (2) business days prior to the Event the Requestor must submit to the DSA a copy of all food permit(s) that are required.</p>\n\n<p>All applicable waivers and permits must be posted and visible onsite during the Event.</p>\n\n<hr/><p><strong>Minors on CSU Property</strong></p>\n\n<p>For the purpose of the present section, \u201c<strong>Minor(s)</strong>\u201d means any person or people under the age of eighteen (18) who is/are not a student(s) of the University.
