March.</p>\n<p>Tasks:</p>\n<ul>\n<li>Organize and oversee CUJAH\u2019s 7th annual Undergraduate Art History Conference</li>\n<li>Create budget, organize room rental, contact speakers, secure #000080;"><strong>Job Title:\xa0Communications Coordinator</strong></span></p>\n<p>The Communications Coordinator is responsible for promoting CUJAH and all of CUJAH\u2019s activities throughout the
