As a union, CUFA\u2019s principal functions are to negotiate and to ensure the application of the Collective Agreement on a day-to-day basis.. We normally negotiate a Collective Agreement every 3 to 5 years. Ensuring that our members\u2019 rights as defined under the Collective Agreement and the law are protected is an on-going activity. We deal with issues involving workload, disciplinary matters, discrimination, harassment, denial of contract renewal, denial of tenure, denial of promotion, and termination. To do this work we can count on the hard work of our grievance officers, our in-house lawyer and our outside counsel.</p>\n<p>We hold membership in the F\xe9d\xe9ration Qu\xe9b\xe9coise des Professeures et Professeurs d\u2019Universit\xe9 (FQPPU) and the Canadian Association of University Teachers (CAUT), which both represent our interests at the provincial, federal, and international levels.  We are also members of the CAUT Defence Fund, which provides material and moral support in the event of a strike.</p>\n<p>The CUFA offices are located in HB-109 on the Loyola campus.</p>\n<p><strong>We want to hear from you!
